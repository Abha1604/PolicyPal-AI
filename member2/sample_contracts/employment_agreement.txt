EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between Bright Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
("Employer") and the individual named in Schedule A ("Employee").

SECTION 3 — TERM
This agreement shall remain in effect for a period of 24 months from the Effective
Date of 1 January 2026, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 14.

SECTION 6 — COMPENSATION
The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of ₹80,000, payable on the last
working day of each month.

SECTION 14 — TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement by providing 90 days' written notice.
The Employer may terminate immediately for cause as defined in Schedule B.

SECTION 18 — TRAINING EXPENSE RECOVERY
If the Employee voluntarily resigns before completing 18 months of continuous
service, the Employee shall be required to repay ₹80,000 in training expenses
incurred by the Employer, to be deducted from final settlement or recovered
directly.

SECTION 21 — CONFIDENTIALITY
The Employee agrees to keep all proprietary information confidential both during
employment and indefinitely after termination of this agreement.

SECTION 24 — NON-COMPETE
For a period of 12 months following termination of employment for any reason,
the Employee shall not accept employment with, or provide services to, any
company that directly competes with the Employer's business.
